“I'm the Whether Man, not the Weather Man, for after all it's more important to know whether there will be weather than what the weather will be.” ― Norton Juster, The Phantom Tollbooth

Site Name

The domain name "thewetherman.com" is avalible for this site. This is a short common phrase turned into a pun. It will be easy for the target audiance to remember.

Purpose

Be a source for children and youth to learn about weather. It is a place of FUN, LEARNING, and EXPLORING. It will show the curent local weather in several cities. This site will also give additional information about specific types of wether and what causes it. Much of the layout will feature a question and answer format. This will will inspire young minds to be curious and seek learning.
